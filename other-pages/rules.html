Here are some ground rules for you demons:

  1. Do not reveal that this is the Bad Place. In order for the experiment to work, the humans must think that they are in the actual Good Place.
  2. Do not take off your skin suits. Obviously, this would shatter the illusion that this is the Good Place
  3. No biting! Not even a little nibble, Gunnar.

See that heavenly light? If you break these rules, Michael will lock you in a room filled with cuddly kittens and force you to listen to Beethoven and look at that light all day, you fat dinks.
