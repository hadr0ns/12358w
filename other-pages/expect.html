What to expect as a demon in this bold new plan:

This kind of torture is a little bit different than what you are probably used to. Instead of physical butthole spiders and bees with teeth, we will be mentally and emotionally torturing the gross humans to repay them for all the crappy things they did on Earth.

Your average day of torture will include:

  • Subtly reminding the humans of how terrible they are
  • Participating in neigborhood events designed to make them miserable
  • Playing the part of a character designed to torture at least one of the humans with your personality
  • When you are not on duty, chilling in the break room, which will be playing relaxing death metal and will have pig urine on tap

Michael thanks you for your participation and promises that this will be just as satisfying of a torture experience as shoving hot dogs in all of their orifices.
